About Paws to Care

Finding the right puppy can be doggone hard work. Paws to Care provides a convenient and efficient means of selecting and purchasing the perfect puppy from the comfort of your home, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Take advantage of our PuppySearch or leisurely browse hundreds of breed profiles and for sale listings with photos, ratings and detailed descriptions. Our goal is to make finding a puppy as enjoyable as owning one.
